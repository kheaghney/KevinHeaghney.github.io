Before taking this leap of faith I was working in Construction and blue collar jobs for many years. Happiness with what I was doing was non existent so I decided enough is enough. I've always felt I had more to offer, but at the time didn't know what that was. My creativeness, eagerness to learn and curiosity would lead me down a path that was somewhat foreign to me. I've since invested everything I have into learning and developing my skills in Web Development/Design. It was the greatest decision I've ever made and has allowed me to find my true passion. I've completed courses through W3Schools and Udacity, where I received my Front End Development Nanodegree. My projects consist of a memory game, arcade game, clock, calculator and a map. I have working experience creating eCommerce Bookstores for a number of Colleges and Universities. I've also created an eCommerce site for a startup toffee company. My journey is ongoing but I hope to find a position to utilize my talent and continue my growth. What sets me apart from the rest is how I managed to get here and I hope to use my drive and passion wherever I work.
